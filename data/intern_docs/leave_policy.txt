Leave and Attendance Policy (General)

This policy applies to all employees including interns and full-time staff at the associate level.

Types of Leave
1. Casual Leave - short notice leave for personal matters, limited to 6 days per year.
2. Sick Leave - requires notification to manager on the same day, medical certificate needed if absence exceeds 2 consecutive days.
3. Public Holidays - the company observes 10 public holidays per year as per the official holiday calendar published by HR each January.

Applying for Leave
Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 2 working days in advance, except for sick leave. Approval is required from your direct manager before the leave is considered confirmed.

Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week with prior manager approval. Remote work requests should be logged in the team calendar.

Late Arrivals
Arriving more than 30 minutes late without prior notice on 3 or more occasions in a month may result in a discussion with your manager during the monthly check-in.
