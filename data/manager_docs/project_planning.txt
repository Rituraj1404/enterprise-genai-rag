Project Planning and Sprint Process

This document describes how managers run sprint planning and resource allocation for their teams.

Sprint Cadence
The engineering org runs on 2-week sprints. Sprint planning happens every other Monday, with retrospectives on the Friday before the next sprint begins.

Resource Allocation
Managers are responsible for balancing workload across team members, ensuring no single engineer is overloaded across more than 2 active projects at a time. Intern tasks should be scoped to allow for learning time and code review cycles.

Cross-Team Dependencies
When a project depends on another team's deliverables, managers must raise this in the weekly cross-team sync and document the dependency in the project's Confluence page under "Risks and Dependencies".

Roadmap Reviews
Quarterly roadmap reviews are held with Directors to align team priorities with company OKRs. Managers present completed work, upcoming milestones, and any resourcing gaps.

On-Call Rotation
Managers maintain the on-call rotation schedule for their team, ensuring at least 2 engineers are trained on incident response procedures at all times. Interns are not included in on-call rotations.

Promotion Nominations
Managers can nominate team members for promotion during the bi-annual calibration cycle. Nominations require a written justification with examples of impact and peer feedback.
