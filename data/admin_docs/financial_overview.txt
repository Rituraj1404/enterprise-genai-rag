Company Financial Overview (Confidential - Admin Only)

This document contains sensitive financial information restricted to senior leadership and administrators.

Annual Revenue
The company reported total annual revenue of $48.2 million for the last fiscal year, representing a 22% year-over-year growth driven primarily by enterprise contract renewals.

Profit Margins
Net profit margin stood at 14.6%, with operating expenses accounting for 61% of total revenue. The largest expense categories were salaries and benefits (58% of operating costs) and cloud infrastructure (17%).

Salary Bands
- Intern: $1,200 - $1,800 per month stipend, varies by location.
- Software Engineer (L1-L2): $65,000 - $95,000 annual base.
- Senior Engineer (L3-L4): $110,000 - $150,000 annual base.
- Engineering Manager: $140,000 - $180,000 annual base, plus performance bonus up to 15%.

Departmental Budget Allocation
Engineering: 45% of total operating budget.
Sales and Marketing: 28%.
G&A (General and Administrative): 15%.
R&D: 12%.

Funding and Investment
The company closed a Series C funding round of $30 million last year, led by a growth equity firm, bringing total funding to date to $72 million.

Profit Sharing
A portion of annual profits, up to 5% of net income, is allocated to the employee bonus pool, distributed based on individual and team performance ratings.
