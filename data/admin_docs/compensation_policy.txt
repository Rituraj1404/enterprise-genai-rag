Compensation and Salary Review Policy (Confidential - Admin Only)

This document is restricted to HR administrators and executive leadership.

Annual Salary Reviews
Salary reviews occur once per year in April, based on performance ratings from the annual review cycle. Average merit increases range from 3% to 8%, with top performers (rated "Exceeds Expectations" or above) eligible for increases up to 15%.

Internal Pay Equity
The company conducts an annual pay equity audit to ensure no statistically significant gaps exist across gender or ethnicity for employees in equivalent roles and levels. Any identified gaps must be remediated within the same fiscal year.

Executive Compensation
C-level executives receive a base salary plus equity grants vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff. Executive bonuses are tied to company-wide revenue and profit targets approved by the board.

Stock Option Pool
The employee stock option pool currently represents 12% of fully diluted shares, reserved for new hires, promotions, and refresh grants for high performers.

Severance Policy
In the event of layoffs, severance is calculated as 2 weeks of base salary per year of service, with a minimum of 4 weeks, plus continuation of health benefits for 60 days.

Confidentiality Notice
All compensation data is strictly confidential. Sharing salary band information, individual compensation details, or budget figures with employees outside of HR and senior leadership is a violation of company policy.
